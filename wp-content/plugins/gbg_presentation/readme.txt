Instructions

1) Add new group
	- Log in admin panel
	- Click on GBG -> Add Group
	- Enter information, Fields with red line is mandatory to fill.
	- Enter company name in the search field, and after few seconds you will get all registrated companies, which contains the word, you have just entered
	- To add company to the group, just click on its name, you will se that the company appeared on the right column "Companies on the group"
	- You can add as many companies, you need. After you are done, click on "Add New Group"
	- If you go to GBG, you will se your new group. On the site, it appears by the country, you added it to.
